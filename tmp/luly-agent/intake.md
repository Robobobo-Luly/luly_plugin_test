# Intake — boardwalk-onchain-overview

## Intent
An internal-prep course for the Luly team to learn what Boardwalk (useboardwalk.com) actually is — a permissionless launchpad protocol with built-in locked liquidity, transparent on-contract fees, anti-sniper mechanics, and BMX-staked community governance — and then to map those product surfaces to concrete Luly offers. The course should leave the reader able to (a) explain Boardwalk in one minute, (b) talk fluently about an Express vs Advanced launch, BMX staking, and Café Boardwalk, and (c) name three Luly product hooks (academy, onboarding, marketing campaigns) that fit Boardwalk's stack.

## Audience
Luly team members preparing to pitch Boardwalk — comfortable with crypto basics but new to Boardwalk specifically.

## Tone
Briefing-style: clear, concrete, light. Confident without crypto-bro buzzwords. Short sentences, plain English.

## Preset
academy-course
Rationale: Standalone teaching unit — a focused course on one topic ("what Boardwalk is + where Luly fits"), not a multi-screen campaign or a waitlist.

## Brand
Company: Boardwalk
Website: https://www.useboardwalk.com/
Voice: Beach/boardwalk metaphor, approachable, transparent ("built by design", "Onchain Starts Here", "There's a role for everyone").

## Materials
- https://www.useboardwalk.com/ — official landing; Express (24h, 10 ETH grad, single fee recipient) vs Advanced (7d, up to 5 recipients, supply vesting); locked liquidity at graduation; fees written into the token contract; anti-sniper fees (40% → standard over ~90 min); 7-day cliff before claims; Café Boardwalk forum auto-created per launch; BMX staking → weekly winner-takes-all "Designated Fees" vote.
